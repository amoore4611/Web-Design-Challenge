Humidity

Due to variability of the data, no correlation can be seen bewteen humidty and latitude. However, some observations can be made. Consistent higher humidity (>60%) can be observed between 40 Deg. to 60 Deg. latitude. This could be attributed to the Arabian Sea which is located around 65 Deg. latitude. Humidity is caused by the amount of water vapor in the air and is typically higher by coastlines due to winds and currents moving heat around.
